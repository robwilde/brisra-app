Help and Info

Thanks for using our App. Here is some quick and easy steps to help us get to you ASAP

1. You can click Find Me to center the map to your location.
2. Pinch to zoom or use the + & - to zoom in and out. Now you can hold the marker and fine tune your location. You can switch from map to a satellite view using the buttons in the top right corner of the map.
3 Press Save Me to send us your location. We Will confirm your location and advise ETA.
